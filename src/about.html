About the Calculator Web App

Welcome to the Calculator Web App, your go-to tool for performing basic and advanced calculations with ease. Designed with a user-friendly interface and intuitive navigation, our calculator app aims to make your daily calculations straightforward and efficient.

Enjoy a seamless experience with our Dashboard and Calculator pages. The Dashboard provides an overview and quick access to features, while the Calculator page is dedicated to performing calculations.

Modern Design

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the app features a clean and responsive design that ensures smooth performance on both desktop and mobile devices.

Versatile Calculations

Whether you need to perform simple arithmetic or complex operations, our calculator is equipped to handle a wide range of mathematical functions.
